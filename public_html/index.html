Introduction

Their goal is to "increase the customers' return on data by taking them on a journey to a modern and innovative data culture." - VisionWorks

Why this project?

The description of this project was all I was looking for. Me, being an advocate for climate change awareness, finding an internship where my love for understanding data AND climate change can be combined, is perfect.

Not only would I get to experiment with new tools learned in the past semester, but I was also going to discover a lot more in the process of data analysis.

Concept of the project

The crux of the project was 'How do we react to extreme weather? How do we cope with it? And what does this say about our perception of climate change?'

The project is being made for a fictional business that has goods and services that rely on climate change perception and wants to find out how certain weather phenomena affect their sales and the general sentiment of the customers.

Goal

The goal of the project was to develop AWS-processes to collect tweets reporting about the weather, weather data, and store in such a way that it becomes easy to consult it ad-hoc and visualize it.

With the data, an analysis will be performed using Machine Learning algorithms using Python. Sentiment analysis will be performed on the social media data and based on this, a story can be told.
